What is Fallout?

The Fallout games are a series of post-apocalypse RPGs. The games are heavily influenced by a retro-futuristic style.

Where are the games set?

The games are set in various areas of America after many years conflict which were building up since 2051,where nuclear bombs were fired between China and the USA on October 23, 2077 known as the Great war, causing the destruction of most of the earth.
